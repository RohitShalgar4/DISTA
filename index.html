About DISTA 2K25

DISTA 2K25 is our college's premier annual technical festival that brings together the brightest minds to showcase their talents, innovations, and technical prowess.

This year's event features exciting competitions, workshops, tech talks, and networking opportunities across various engineering disciplines.
